Go Underground

Located in the timeless valley of Pelham, TN, at the base of Monteagle mountain, The Caverns lies in an ancient and almost hidden cove in one of the most cavernous places on Earth.

The Caverns is the world's newest (and oldest) "performance hall". The Caverns features singular acoustics in a sublime and surreal setting. It's a magical cave and we welcome you to dig deeper.

Dive into some common questions most of our guests have, come prepared and get ready for an experience of the lifetime.

Where is The Caverns?

God’s country! The GPS address is The Caverns, 555 Charlie Roberts Road, Pelham, TN 37366.

From Nashville: ake I-24 East for 80 miles, take exit 127 (TN-50 E/US-64 Pelham) at the Stuckeys Store, Continue on TN-50E for 6 miles (when road curves to the left, stay straight), take a left onto Payne Cove Rd, Continue on Payne Cove Rd for 1.5 miles, take a left onto Charlie Roberts Rd, follow Charlie Roberts Rd. for 3 miles to The Caverns.

From Chattanooga: take I-24 W to TN-50 E for 53 miles, take Exit 127 at the Stuckeys Store, Continue on TN-50E for 6 miles (when road curves to the left, stay straight), take a left onto Payne Cove Rd, Continue on Payne Cove Rd for 1.5 miles, take a left onto Charlie Roberts Rd, follow Charlie Roberts Rd. for 3 miles to The Caverns.

From Monteagle: take US-41 N to TN-50 E, Continue on TN-50E for 6 miles (when road curves to the left, stay straight), take a left onto Payne Cove Rd, Continue on Payne Cove Rd for 1.5 miles, take a left onto Charlie Roberts Rd, follow Charlie Roberts Rd. for 3 miles to The Caverns.

From Atlanta: take I-75 N to I-24 W for 168 miles, take exit 127 (TN-50 E/US-64 Pelham) at the Stuckeys Store, Continue on TN-50E for 6 miles (when road curves to the left, stay straight), take a left onto Payne Cove Rd, Continue on Payne Cove Rd for 1.5 miles, take a left onto Charlie Roberts Rd, follow Charlie Roberts Rd. for 3 miles to The Caverns.

How do I get my tickets?

All tickets are emailed to you. Please print your tickets prior to coming to the venue. If you’re unable to print your tickets please go to Will Call located at the Welcome Center at The Caverns. Please check your ticket information for show specific times. In most cases the box office will open two hours prior to doors opening.

How do we get into the cave to see the show at The Caverns?

The showroom is about a 1/2 mile walk from the parking lot along a gravel path. Once at the cave mouth the walk is not difficult but plan to come a little early if you have difficulty walking. Most guests can do the walk without much trouble. Limited handicap seating is available for most shows. If you need handicap seating, please be sure to select those seats when you purchase tickets.

Are there restrooms at the show?

Yes the cave is equipped with full bathroom facilities.

Are there concessions available at The Caverns?

Yes, we have some delicious, locally sourced cave fare that is only available at The Caverns. Adult beverages too!

Can we bring our own food and drinks to The Caverns?

You can enjoy your munchies and drinks in the parking lot, but food and drinks are not allowed outside of the parking lot. Food and drink are available for purchase inside the cave.

May we bring our own chairs?

Nope. When you buy your ticket you will receive your specific seat. All seats have cushions and at the Caverns we know that padded seats make for happy fannies.

How long are the shows?

Show lengths vary from show to show but are generally 2-3 hours.

Can I bring my animal to the show?

We love animals as much you but unfortunately for the safety and comfort of all guests pets are not allowed at The Caverns. Working service animals are welcome at The Caverns. Service animals must be wearing proper identification and/or individuals must have proof of certification or a license for the service animal. The ADA defines a service animal as any guide dog, signal dog, or other animal individually trained to provide assistance to an individual with a disability.

What items are prohibited at The Caverns?

In the interest of providing a safe environment for all our guests, artists, and staff, The Caverns has established a list of items prohibited inside the venue and on the property:

All weapons (with or without a permit), Aerosols, Audio recording devices (unless special written permission is granted), Beach balls, Bull horns, Cameras with telephoto or removable zoom lenses, Cans, bottles, Confetti, glitter (any items that may be thrown), Drones or any other remote controlled moving object, Fireworks, Flammable Liquids, Explosive materials, Hula hoops, Illegal drugs, Juggling sticks, Laptops/tablets, Laser pens, Masks (and face paint), Monopods, Musical instruments, Noisemakers, Outside alcohol, Outside food and beverage, Oversized bags (bags over 12” x 6” x 12” not allowed, all bags subject to security screening), Pepper/mace spray, Selfie sticks, Totems/walking sticks, Tripods, Video cameras, Any other item deemed inappropriate by management/staff.

The Prohibited items list is subject to change. At the direction of the General Manager, items may be added to, or removed from the Prohibited Items List. The Caverns may deny entry to, or remove, any individual in possession of Prohibited List items (or any item deemed inappropriate by management/staff), at any time before, during, and after an event. We want everyone to be safe & enjoy the show!

I purchased a package, what do I do now?

Once you purchase your package sit back, relax and let us do the rest! Watch your email for your show specific package details.

I purchased a package, how will I get my great seats up close to the stage?

If you’ve purchased a Stay & Cave, Deep Dive or Underground package, sit back & get ready to enjoy your favorite artist at The Caverns. We will reserve great seats for you. Please see your ticket and confirmation email for specific seat info. Not all shows will offer packages.

I want to get deeper into The Caverns, can I take a cave tour to see the "Wild" sections of the caves?

The Caverns is a part of a large underground cave system with beautiful large rooms, stalactites, stalagmites, an underground lost river and much more. We currently do not offer guided cave tours but are working on opening sections of the cave system to visitors in the near future. For your safety and that of others, we ask that all guests remain in designated areas. Please do not hike through the woods or venture outside of our paths.

What time zone is The Caverns in?

We are on Central Time.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 15 or more are eligible for group rates with great benefits. Please email info@bluegrassunderground.com with the number of people attending and the show you’d like to attend. Payments for group tickets must be received 30 days prior to the show date. Select shows may not be eligible for group rates, and group rates are not available for the PBS tapings.

What is the Camera/Photo policy at The Caverns?

We highly encourage you to take pictures while in the cave of anything you like, but please no flash photography during the show. Please take lots of pictures and post to your social media & share your trip with friends & family. Digital, disposable or 35mm cameras are allowed at The Caverns. Professional cameras with detachable or telephoto lenses are prohibited.

All photos taken on The Caverns property are not be used for resale or commercial use unless a Locations/Property Release has been granted by the venue management. The Caverns reserves the right to reuse all images & video recorded on the property for promotional use eith no payment to you.

The Caverns reserves the right to restrict all video and photography based on the request of artists. See your show specific ticket for details about photography at your show.

Flash photography is prohibited during all performances. Unless otherwise posted or announced, videotaping/filming of performances is prohibited at The Caverns except by approved media. Failure to comply may result in ejection from the venue & the property.

Use hashtags "#thecaverns" and/or "#bluegrassunderground" & tag us when possible. Please respect those around & behind you and do not block anyone’s view.

Can my band or my friends band play at The Caverns?

We strive to present the highest level of quality music to our patrons. One way we do that is by only working with artists and bands that we have seen live ourselves. We will also often work with booking agents and management that we have a long-standing, working relationship with. With that being said we are also looking for new artists, bands & configurations in all genres. Our team of music lovers often attend regional concerts, music festivals & music industry conferences, we are always on the lookout for a great new artists. You can always contact us via email at info@bluegrassunderground.com though we normally only book as described in the process above. Thanks for your support and interest in performing at The Caverns.

What is the weather like in the cave?

The cave is a comfortable temperature for most but you are in the cave so please bring a light jacket or a sweater in case you get chilly. If the forecast is calling for rain so please bring a raincoat for walking from the box office to the cave.
